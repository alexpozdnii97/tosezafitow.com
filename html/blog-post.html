Exercise Motivation: 7 Tips For Staying Motivated

Exercise is good for us, and we all know it. Exercising can help keep our bones and cardiovascular system healthy, boost our energy, increase our metabolism, and help us lose or maintain weight .....

How To Lose Weight Safely

Of course, you can lose weight quickly. There are plenty of diets that allow you to lose pounds quickly; however, they leave you feeling deprived and hungry. The best way to lose weight is to do it slowly and safely, in order to keep the pounds off permanently . You surely don’t want to lose weight and, after a while, regain it.

1. Eat More Vegetables and Fruits

Vegetables and fruits are weight-loss-friendly and extremely healthy foods. In addition to being high in fiber, nutrients, and water, they also have very low energy density. What this means is that you won’t consume too many calories when you’re eating large servings of vegetables and fruits. Studies have concluded that people who eat more vegetables and fruits typically weigh less.

2. Eat Slowly

When you’re eating food, chew deliberately and make sure that you taste each bit that is going into your mouth. You should swallow once you’ve chewed up all of the food, make sure that you chew slowly, and repeat the process after you’ve swallowed. Not only will the food be enjoyed more properly by eating slowly, but it will also give you cues when you’ve had enough, and you can stop.

3. Trim Portions

Most of the portions served at home and in restaurants are bigger than what you need. You will lose weight if you do nothing else but reduce the size of your potions by 10% to 20%. To get an understanding of the usual sizes of your potions, get out a measuring cup, and work on trimming them. You can also control your portion instantly by using small cups, plates, and bows.
